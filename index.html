About - The NCC

The NITER Computer Club (NCC) is a dynamic student organization at NITER that aims to foster technical skills and innovation. It serves as a platform for students to explore various aspects of computing, organize exciting events, and participate in workshops that enhance their technical abilities. Through hands-on learning and collaborative activities, NCC helps members stay up-to-date with the latest technology trends and prepares them for future challenges in the tech industry.
